Why FluxFrame Pro?

FluxFrame Pro isn’t just a cockpit—it’s a universal platform engineered for professionals, enthusiasts, and enterprises. From flight schools to eSports teams, our rugged, modular design adapts to every scenario.

  • Limitless Modularity: Quick-release mounts, universal rails, and VESA adapters let you swap yokes, wheels, and displays in seconds.
  • Ergonomic Mastery: Precision-adjustable rails and seat mounts ensure optimal posture and comfort, even through marathon sessions.
  • Immersive Visualization: Supports up to four curved ultra-wide panels (270°+), VR bases, and motion actuators for next-level realism.
  • Rock-Solid Stability: Heavy-gauge aluminum frame, rated to 1,000 kg, resists flex, vibration, and drift under any load.
