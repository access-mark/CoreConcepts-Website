FluxFrame Pro

Executive Overview

FluxFrame Pro is the next-generation simulation cockpit for professionals, enterprises, and enthusiasts. With rock-solid stability and tool-free modularity, it adapts instantly to training, R&D, eSports, and immersive workspaces.

Design Philosophy

  • Adaptability: Tool-free mounts transform your cockpit in minutes.
  • Durability: Industrial-grade aluminum frame rated to 1 000 kg.
  • Immersion: Supports VR, motion actuators, and a 270° field of view.

Service Breakdown

  • Universal Rail System: Slide any accessory anywhere.
  • Ergo Tuning: ±5 mm precision seat and pedal adjustments.
  • Display Ecosystem: Four 34″ ultrawides + overhead HUD.
  • Power & Data: Built-in USB 3.1, CAT 6, 12 V daisy-chain.
  • Motion & VR Ready: Pre-wired for actuators and tracking.
  • Enterprise Support: On-site setup and 2 year warranty.
