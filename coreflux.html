FluxFrame Pro

Executive Overview

FluxFrame Pro is the next-generation simulation cockpit built for professionals, enterprises, and enthusiasts. With rock-solid stability, tool-free modularity, and full VR/motion readiness, it adapts instantly to flight training, R&D, eSports, and immersive workspaces.

Expanded Service Breakdown

  • Modular Mounting System: Quick-release rails for yokes, wheels, joysticks, and control panels—swap devices in seconds.
  • Ergonomic Precision: Seat height ±200 mm, slide ±300 mm, tilt ±15°—designed for marathon comfort.
  • Multi-Display Support: Up to 4× curved ultrawide panels (270°+) plus overhead HUD mount.
  • Cable Management: Integrated channels and zip-tie rails keep wiring tidy and out of sight.
  • VR & Motion Ready: Pre-wired ports for actuators, tracking sensors, and haptic devices.
  • Industrial Strength: 6061-T6 aluminum frame rated to 1,000 kg for zero flex under any load.
