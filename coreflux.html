FluxFrame Pro

Executive Overview

FluxFrame Pro is the next-generation sim cockpit for professionals, enterprises, and enthusiasts. With rock-solid stability, tool-free modularity, and full VR/motion readiness, it adapts instantly to training, R&D, eSports, and immersive workspaces.

Expanded Service Breakdown

  • Modular Mounting: Quick-release rails for yokes, wheels, panels.
  • Ergonomic Precision: Seat ±200 mm, slide ±300 mm, tilt ±15°.
  • Multi-Display: Up to 4× curved ultrawides + overhead HUD.
  • Cable Management: Concealed channels keep cables tidy.
  • VR & Motion: Pre-wired for actuators and haptic sensors.
  • Industrial Strength: 1 000 kg-rated 6061-T6 aluminum frame.
